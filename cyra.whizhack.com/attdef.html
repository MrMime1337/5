Attack and Defense

RED Team

A Red team or team Red is a group that plays the role of an enemy or competitor to provide security feedback from that perspective. Red teams mimicks real-world hackers and are used to test the strength of any organization.

Blue Team

A Blue team or team Blue is a group of individuals who perform an analysis of information systems to ensure security, identify security flaws, verify the effectiveness of each security measure, and make certain all security measures are in place.

Attack and Defence Concept

A group of the Red team and the Blue team of an organization come together to attack and defend the virtually emulated scenario which mimics the original infrastructure and their critical assets, that helps the organization to empower its sturdiness against foreign attacks. This helps in learning of all the team members for a competitive and cooperative mindset while preparing them for quick responses to any unwanted incident. Whizrange provides a bunch of possible Scenarios which may occur in the real world that help any organization to train and practice.
